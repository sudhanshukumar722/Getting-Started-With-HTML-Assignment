Tag:
Tags are the starting and ending parts of an HTML element. 
They begin with < symbol and end with > symbol. Whatever written inside < and > are called tags.
Element:
HTML elements are defined by a starting tag, may contain some content and a closing tag.
For example, <h1>Heading 1</h1> is a HTML element but just <h1> is a starting tag and </h1> is a closing tag.
